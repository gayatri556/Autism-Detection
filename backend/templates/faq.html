Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool a medical diagnosis?

No. This is a screening tool intended to help identify potential signs of autism. Please consult a licensed professional for a formal diagnosis.

Who can use this tool?

Any parent, guardian, or caregiver who wants a quick, simple, and private way to screen their child for early behavioral signs of autism.

How accurate is the result?

The prediction is based on a machine learning model trained on public data. While it's reasonably accurate, it should never replace clinical evaluation.
